# logpro-delivery-app-android

The app will be used by the motorcycle courier. It is through the app that the motorcycle courier will receive deliveries, accepting them or not. By accepting, he receives the the address of the establishment that made the request, the request products and the final recipients adress (customers of the establishment). Also, with the app, the motorcycle courier can view his profile, view the historic of his deliveries and get in touch with the LogPro central
